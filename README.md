Simple. Transparent. Secure.

Most dues systems answer one question: *who paid?*
NexisPay answers three: "who paid, who hasn't, and where the money actually went."

Built for Nexis but designed for any student group, department, or community organization tired of chasing payments in a group chat and keeping receipts in a notebook.

---

##  The Pitch

Every organization collects dues. Almost none show members what those dues *built*.

NexisPay turns a payment form into a transparency platform. Members don't just pay, they watch their contributions fund real projects, earn recognition for showing up early and consistently, and always know exactly where they stand. Treasurers and admins get a real financial command center instead of a spreadsheet. Nothing here is a mockup — every number on every screen is live, pulled straight from the database as it happens.

# What It Does

#For Members
- One-tap registration with an auto-generated Member ID (`NEX-2026-001`, `NEX-2026-002`...)
- Pay dues instantly: via Paystack — card or mobile money
- Auto-generated receipts: for every payment, printable/savable as PDF
- Payment history: full record, always available
- Community Impact Board: see exactly which projects member dues are funding, and how close each one is to fully funded
- Achievement badges: Early Bird, Consistent Contributor, Gold Supporter, Fully Paid Up — all computed live from real payment behavior, nothing hardcoded
- Live announcements: from treasurers/admins, right in the dashboard
- Google sign-in" or classic email/password, plus self-serve password reset

### For Treasurers
- Real-time collection totals, paid/unpaid breakdown, monthly trends
- Full payment ledger across every member
- Manage dues categories and community project funding
- Post announcements, review pending approvals, export CSV reports
- (Deliberately can't promote/demote member roles or delete accounts that stays admin-only, enforced at the database level, not just hidden in the UI)

##  Security Highlights

- Row Level Security on every table: members can only ever see their own payments; only staff (treasurer/admin) can see everyone's
- Role changes are enforced by a database trigger, not just a hidden button — even a direct API call can't let a treasurer self-promote to admin
- Member IDs and receipt numbers are generated by the database itself, never trusted from the client
